Story

Born where salt air meets patient craft.

THRIWANA began at a wooden table near the coast, with leftover cloth, inherited tools, and long evenings of hand-finishing. What started as gifts for friends became a small studio rooted in rhythm rather than rush.

We work in small batches, listening to season, material, and mood. Each piece carries the hand of its maker: a slight variation, a softened edge, a detail that remembers touch.

Sizing & Details

Fit notes and thoughtful finishing.

  • Sizes: XS, S, M, L, XL (select styles available in custom measurements).
  • Fit: Most silhouettes are relaxed. If between sizes, choose down for a closer fit.
  • Materials: Linen, cotton, and selected deadstock fabrics with breathable hand-feel.
  • Shipping: Pieces are prepared in 3–7 days before dispatch.
  • Handmade variation: Slight differences in stitch and tone are natural and part of the story.
